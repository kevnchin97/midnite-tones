<idea>
**Design Movement**: Brutalist Editorial Photography Portfolio

**Core Principles**:
1. Raw, unadorned presentation — the photography speaks for itself
2. Fixed left sidebar navigation with minimal typographic treatment
3. Two-column masonry grid that respects original image proportions
4. Warm beige background that recedes behind the imagery

**Color Philosophy**: Warm beige `#DFDDD8` background (matching original rgb(223,221,216)) with near-black `#1a1a1a` text and muted gray `#a3a3a3` for inactive nav links. The palette is deliberately neutral to not compete with the photography.

**Layout Paradigm**: Fixed 180px left sidebar with logo at top and vertical nav list. Main content area fills remaining width with a two-column masonry grid. Images are flush with no border-radius, separated by minimal gaps.

**Signature Elements**:
1. HH monogram logo in decorative serif style at top of sidebar
2. Masonry grid with images of varying heights creating organic rhythm
3. Thumbnail strip at bottom for gallery navigation

**Interaction Philosophy**: Hover states are subtle — nav links lighten slightly. Images have no hover effects to maintain editorial purity.

**Animation**: Minimal — only fade-in on page load. No transitions on hover.

**Typography System**: Proxima Nova (approximated with Nunito or similar) for nav links at 13px, lowercase. Logo uses a decorative serif/display treatment.
</idea>
